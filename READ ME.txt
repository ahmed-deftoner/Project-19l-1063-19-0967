######## SPACE INVADERS ##########
 
INSTRUCTIONS:-
1- press arrow keys for movement.
2- press spacebar to shoot aliens.
3- the console window settings are according to my PC's screen, if the shooter isn't vsible, adjust the width and height variables in the code manually for your computer screen.
4- you have 3 lives, you lose 1 life if an alien passes the shooter, you lose all lives if you get hit by the alien's bullets.
5- the score is displayed at the end.
